“SOMEBODY HAS BEEN AT MY PORRIDGE!” said the great huge bear, in his great huge voice. “Somebody has been at my porridge!” said the middle bear, in his middle voice. Then the little, small wee bear looked at his, and there was the spoon in the porridge pot, but the porridge was all gone. “Somebody has been at my porridge, and has eaten it all up!” said the little, small wee bear, in his little, small wee voice.
